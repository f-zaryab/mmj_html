How it works

An easier way to get your MMJ licence.

Step 1

Take a free pre-assessment

Find out if you qualify for an MMJ card by answering a few simple questions. Once completed, schedule a virtual appointment with a licensed MMJ doctor.

Step 2

Attend your virtual appointment with an MMJ doctor

Log in to your patient profile from a mobile device or desktop and join your virtual appointment with a licensed MMJ doctor. Virtual appointments last approximately 10-15 minutes.

Step 3

Receive recommendation and apply to state registry

After you receive your MMJ recommendation, we will guide you to the state’s registry. There, you will pay the registration fee (typically $50), apply to the state registry, and receive your MMJ card.
